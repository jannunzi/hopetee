More than a shirt

HopeTee was born from the desire to support the people affected by the devastating earthquakes in Venezuela — turning a simple T-shirt into a meaningful gift.

Every piece is thoughtfully designed for the person who receives it. Not a mass-produced garment with no story behind it, but a unique creation made with sensitivity, artistic expression, and purpose — carrying a message of hope, dignity, and compassion.

Renowned Venezuelan artists — illustrators, designers, cartoonists, painters, musicians, writers and more — are voluntarily donating their talent to transform each T-shirt into a message of hope.

Every HopeTee is given completely free to residents and volunteer first responders in the affected areas.
"We don't want the recipient to feel they are receiving a donation. We want them to feel that someone thought about them — and that they are not alone."
